### Notes

**Baseline Scan ($99)** is a scan report, not a PAR. No registry record. No seal. No Access Proof Packet. It is a lead-generation and diagnostic tool. Results show what a full audit would find; they do not constitute a conformance claim. Use for: initial assessment, pre-audit screening, developer feedback loops.

**Standard ($1,500)** is the entry PAR tier. Produces a registry record, a sealed Access Proof Packet, and an embeddable badge. Appropriate for SMBs, open-source projects, smaller government properties, and vendors needing a baseline proof record.

**Professional ($4,500)** is the primary commercial tier. Full Access Proof Packet, seal, registry record with scope summary, and targeted human review on identified risk areas. Appropriate for enterprise vendor certification, public entity readiness, mid-size SaaS.

**Expert ($9,500)** is the L2 tier with named external co-signer. Required for procurement contexts where a single-party self-assertion is insufficient. Appropriate for regulated industries, federal procurement, large public entities, and AI interface review.

**Assurance ($25K–$150K/yr)** is the enterprise continuous monitoring tier. Variable based on subject scope and monitoring cadence. Priced per engagement.
