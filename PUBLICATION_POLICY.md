# A11yGate Publication Policy
_Version: 0.1_
_Status: Canonical — legal review recommended before publishing first named failure_

---

## Purpose

A11yGate may publish registry statuses — not private evidence dumps.
Public reporting must be scoped, factual, and reviewable.

The goal of publishing failures is accountability, not punishment.
The goal of publishing passes is recognition, not advertising.

---

## What Is Published

**Always published:**
- Pass/fail aggregate statistics (quarterly)
- Named verified organizations (with consent or by default for public entities)
- Registry status changes (upon notice period)

**Published after notice:**
- Named conditional → expired transitions
- Named revocations
- Spot check failure outcomes

**Never published:**
- Private evidence
- Detailed barrier logs (unless client authorizes)
- Personal reviewer information
- Dispute correspondence

---

## Notice Requirements

Before publishing a downgrade, revocation, or material failure for an external organization:

**Default notice period:** 30 days
**B0 Access Blocker:** Immediate notice — 10-day remediation window before status update
**Revocation for misrepresentation:** No notice period required

Notice method: contact email on record + registry dashboard notification.

---

## Remediation Windows by Severity

| Severity | Window | Outcome if unresolved |
|---|---|---|
| B0 Access Blocker | 10 days | Seal suspended |
| B1 Severe Barrier | 30 days | Conditional status |
| B2 Material Barrier | 60 days | Conditional status may remain |
| BX Exception | Per exception expiry | Revoked if expired without renewal |

---

## Dispute Process

1. Organization submits dispute evidence (via contact@a11ygate.org)
2. Record marked `under_review`
3. A11yGate reviews within 30 days
4. Outcome: status confirmed, corrected, or removed
5. Dispute notes appended to registry record (summary only — not evidence)

---

## Approved Public Language

Use:
- "Record expired."
- "Conditional pass with documented exceptions."
- "Access blocker identified within stated scope."
- "Verification revoked due to unresolved drift."
- "Spot check found material barriers. Remediation in progress."

Never use:
- "Bad actor."
- "Noncompliant organization."
- "Illegal."
- "Unsafe for disabled users."
- Language that implies legal conclusions.

---

## Self-Attestation vs Verified

Self-attested (Registered) records are subject to spot checks.
If a self-attested record claims scope that doesn't hold under audit:
→ Status updated to `conditional` or `blocked`
→ 30-day notice
→ Organization may upgrade to L2 verified to resolve

---

## Evidence Privacy

Private evidence remains restricted unless:
- Publication is authorized in writing by the organization
- Publication is legally required (e.g. subpoena, regulatory order)
- Evidence is necessary to substantiate a public revocation

This protects A11yGate from evidence disputes while maintaining enforcement authority.
